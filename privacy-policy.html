Information Collection

The Application does not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location. Only vehicle-related information and images are submitted for analysis:

No other personal data is required or collected.

Image Processing

Images submitted through the Application are processed temporarily to detect vehicle faults or defects. Key points:

  • Images are transmitted securely to the analysis API.
  • Images and analysis data are not stored permanently by the Application or API.
  • All processing happens in real-time and is deleted immediately after analysis.
